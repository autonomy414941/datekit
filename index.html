Common Questions

Can I calculate timezone differences?

DateKit calculates based on your browser locale and displays UTC output for Unix conversions. For timezone-to-timezone scheduling, convert each time to Unix first.

Does DateKit support negative date offsets?

Yes. Use a negative number in Add / Subtract Days to move backwards from the base date.

What Unix formats are accepted?

You can input Unix seconds (10 digits) or milliseconds (13 digits). DateKit auto-detects the unit and returns local, UTC, and ISO values.

Is DateKit free to use?

Yes. DateKit runs fully in your browser and is free to use.
